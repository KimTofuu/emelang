Segara Suites

Segara Suites stands as a testament to contemporary elegance, boasting a collection of chic rooms and suites infused with modern Asian design elements, adorned with the intricate details reminiscent of Balinese home decor. Nestled within the vibrant ambiance of Subic Bay Freeport Zone, this three-star gem not only offers a luxurious stay but also serves as a gateway to a plethora of family-friendly attractions and activities. From pristine beaches to exhilarating water sports, guests can effortlessly immerse themselves in the charm of Subic Bay, making Segara Suites an ideal retreat for those seeking both relaxation and adventure. We are located at Segara Suites Main Building, Washington St, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Zambales.
