All Hands Beach

All Hands Beach, nestled between the strategic locations of Subic Bay International Airport and the Subic Container Terminal, is witnessing a transformative phase under the stewardship of Brighterday Subic Ltd., Inc. This idyllic coastal retreat is renowned for its crystal-clear waters that invite both tranquility and leisure. With its serene environment and the gentle lapping of calm waves, it stands as a pristine getaway destination. The ongoing developments by Brighterday Subic are poised to enhance the beach's natural allure, aiming to elevate the visitor experience while maintaining the ecological integrity that makes All Hands Beach a cherished spot among locals and tourists alike



Icove Beach Hotel

Looking for a place to stay in Olongapo? Then look no further than iCove Beach Hotel, a romantic small hotel that brings the best of Olongapo to your doorstep. The small hotel offers a 24 hour front desk, room service, and a coffee shop, to make your visit even more pleasant. The property also features a pool and free breakfast. Guests arriving by vehicle have access to free parking.





Subiza Beach Resort

Subiza Beach Resort is the right choice for visitors who are searching for a combination of charm, peace and quiet, and a convenient position from which to explore Subic Bay's emerald-blue waters and nature. It is an affordable and comfortable hotel, situated along the shorelines. The hotel staff offers an attentive, personalized service and are always available to offer any help to guests. Our 50 air-conditioned guest rooms from Standard and Deluxe categories, all the way up to Superior and Executive rooms offers the amenities of your own home. The hotel's swimming pool overlooks the pristine beach which is a few feet away. We are located at 13 Baloy Long Beach Rd, Barrio Barretto, Olongapo, Zambales.

By The Sea

Nestled along the pristine shores of Olongapo, "By the Sea Olongapo" is a tranquil haven where the gentle rhythm of the ocean soothes the soul. With its picturesque setting and panoramic views of the azure waters, this seaside retreat offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Guests can indulge in luxurious accommodations, elegantly designed to blend modern comforts with coastal charm. Whether lounging by the infinity pool, savoring delectable cuisine at the seaside restaurant, or strolling along the sandy beaches, every moment at "By the Sea Olongapo" is a blissful retreat into serenity, where the symphony of waves provides the ultimate soundtrack to relaxation and rejuvenation. We are located at 99 RH 5 National Hwy, Olongapo, 2200 Zambales.



El Molina Beach Resort

Elmolina Beach Resort is a concealed sanctuary placed along the busy highways of Barrio Barretto, quite popular not only with tourists but locals of Olongapo and Zambales. Offers a tranquil tropical encircling that boasts of tall palm trees, fresh air, calm waters, and native nipa huts that can be rented for day tour where you can enjoy the beach for the whole day and prepare your food by the grill. With over 20 well-made air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities such as cozy bed-setting, coffee table, WiFi access, dresser, bathroom with hot and cold shower, and basic toiletries. The resort also caters to team buildings, family gatherings, birthdays, and group packages customized and cost friendly. Go on a daring adventure by trying out the water sports activities offered such as Jet Ski, kayaking, island hopping, and snorkeling. For a sportier venture, you can also play beach volleyball which is set up near the shores. We are located at 135 RH 5 National Hwy, Olongapo, 2200 Zambales.

Whiterock Waterpark and Beach Hotel looks forward to welcoming you on your visit to Subic Bay Freeport Zone. We are located at Purok 3, Subic, 2209 Zambales.




Adventure Beach Waterpark

Adventure Beach Waterpark in Subic Bay is a must-visit aquatic playground for day-trippers, families, and friends. Start your day with a ride down the rapids at River Bend on custom-designed inflatables, then take on the Twin Twister Slide, a 3-story tower with two twisting slides that plunge into the pool below. Enjoy a glimpse of the vast ocean horizon as you climb. Keep the adrenaline going with the Splashing Seesaw, featuring various water slides, and relax in the wading pools under Rainbow Falls' gentle drizzle. End your day with a golden sunset on the beach. Visit us at Ilanin Road, Camayan Wharf Rd, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, 2222 Republic of the Philippines.
